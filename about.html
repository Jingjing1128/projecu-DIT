Jackson Yee (Chinese: 易烊千玺; pinyin: Yì Yángqiānxǐ, born 28 November 2000) is a Chinese singer, dancer and actor. After a talent manager discovered him at a children talent competition, where he performed a hip-hop dance, and signed to TF Entertainment, Jackson became the youngest member of the Chinese boy band TFBOYS in 2013.
